Proxy

always available, always private, always you.

Proxy is a next-generation personal AI assistant that learns how you communicate and manage tasks. It's like having a highly intelligent partner that's always ready to help, adapting to your unique style for conversations, messages, and decisions. Running entirely on your device, Proxy ensures privacy while seamlessly integrating into your life.

Cutting-Edge AI, Right on Your Device

Leveraging the power of Apple silicon, Proxy processes your data locally, giving you complete control over your privacy. Our advanced inference algorithms ensure high-quality, coherent, and diverse outputs that sound like you. Proxy is optimized to deliver exceptional performance without compromising your data security.

Seamless Integration with Cloud Power

When faced with more complex reasoning tasks, Proxy can securely leverage cloud-based AI services. Our hybrid system ensures that your sensitive data remains local while still leveraging cutting-edge models when needed. It's the best of both worlds: local privacy with cloud capabilities.

Your Personal AI, Evolved

Proxy is not just a tool — it's your reflection. Our advanced personalization mechanisms allow Proxy to adapt to your unique communication style and preferences over time. From understanding your writing style to managing your tasks, Proxy becomes an extension of you, helping you achieve more with less effort.

The Future of Work and Communication

You can delegate tasks and coordinate your efforts like never before. Your Proxy understands context deeply, building complex webs of understanding and memory, which helps you manage multiple jobs simultaneously. It's not just about productivity — it's about amplifying your abilities and reclaiming your time.
