What is Proxy?

Imagine stepping into another place, another body, without leaving your home.

Proxy is new technology that combines high-speed internet, virtual reality, 3D printing, artificial intelligence, and robotics. It lets you interact with the world in a new way: you will be able to drive a physical robot known as a Proxy, from anywhere with internet access. See what they see, hear what they hear, and move them like they're an extension of you.

These devices can be piloted remotely, breaking down barriers and opening up infinite possibilities. By combining technological advancements in several fields, we aim to create an immersive and human experience; both for the person using a Proxy and those interacting with the Proxy in the real world. Your first Proxy will connect you to the physical world, just as the internet connects you to the digital world.

We are building the future of interaction: Proxy can bridge the gap between the physical and digital world.

How would I use Proxy?

Proxy enables you to be anywhere, anytime: you will experience the world like never before.

  • Visit Loved Ones: Easily attend birthdays, holidays, and impromptu get-togethers without the expense of travel.
  • Remote Work: Attend meetings, give presentations, and collaborate with colleagues as if in person.
  • Accessible Experiences: Explore the world from the comfort of your home.
  • Authentic Interactions: Foster genuine connections with people, beyond what's possible online.
  • Immersive Learning: Attend classes and workshops with a sense of presence, engaging with instructors and peers.
  • Outsource Errands: Free up time by having someone else remotely handle your errands and chores.
  • Enhance Mobility: Participate in physical events and activities, even with mobility issues.

Can Proxy deliver on this vision?

Yes. Here's why:

  • The technology is ready: Advancements in virtual reality, robotics, and artificial intelligence have made Proxy not only feasible, but affordable.
  • The need is real: There is a growing demand for ways to bridge the physical and digital world, especially in post-pandemic society.
  • The team is passionate and determined: We are a diverse group of individuals with expertise in various fields, all driven by the potential of Proxy.
  • We are committed to open source: We believe in collaboration and community involvement, which is why we are building Proxy with the same open-source mindset.
  • State-of-the-art tools: AI enhances our team's capabilities, enabling us to exceed expectations and achieve ambitious goals with minimal bureaucracy and complexity.

What's next?

Our team is committed to achieving more with less, and we've taken a focused approach to development, prioritizing an interactive demo by Summer 2024. Step by step, we are bringing Proxy to life.
