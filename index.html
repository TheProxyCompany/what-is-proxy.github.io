Be There, From Anywhere

Proxy is a telepresence robotics company building a future where you can be anywhere, anytime.

What We're Building

We're developing a platform that combines immersive VR, advanced robotics, and AI to enable a new level of remote presence and interaction. Our two core products:

Proxy Robot

A modular and customizable robot designed to navigate various environments, controlled remotely by a human pilot through an immersive VR interface.

Proxy Terminal

A user-friendly device that connects to a VR headset, acting as a communication hub for real-time, low-latency control of the Proxy Robot.

Our Vision

Imagine attending a meeting across the globe from your living room, exploring a museum from your wheelchair, or collaborating with colleagues on a project as if you were in the same room, no matter the distance. Proxy is making this a reality, bringing the power of physical presence to anyone, anywhere.

Why Proxy?

We're not just building robots; we're building a platform for connection and empowerment. We believe in a future where technology enhances human capabilities, breaks down barriers, and makes the impossible possible.
