What is Proxy?

Proxy is an affordable, VR-controlled robot that lets you be present in any location, as if you were actually there. See what it sees, hear what it hears, and move it with intuitive ease. Break free from the limitations of your physical body and experience the world like never before.

How will Proxy make me happier?

  • Ditch the Commute: Say goodbye to traffic jams and hello to more free time.
  • Explore the World: Discover new places, cultures, and adventures without leaving your couch.
  • Connect with Others: Be there for all the important moments, big or small, no matter the distance.
  • Reimagine Possible: Participate in activities you never thought possible.

Why Proxy?

We're a team of builders, inspired by Proxy's potential to revolutionize the way we live, work, and connect, and we're dedicated to making it happen. By combining a "do more with less" philosophy with the purposeful use of AI, we are moving faster than entire research labs and building more with less.

We are confident that the ability to control robots remotely in real-time has the potential to revolutionize countless industries. If you are interested in integrating Proxy's technology into your products, please contact us: contact@what-is-proxy.com
