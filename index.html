Proxy

Always Available, Always Private, Always You

Proxy is your personal AI that runs locally on your devices. Designed to learn your unique communication style and preferences, Proxy handles emails, messages, and routine tasks when you're unavailable or focusing on what matters most. Operating entirely on your device, Proxy ensures your privacy while seamlessly integrating into your daily life.

Cutting-Edge AI, Right on Your Device

Powered by the latest technology, Proxy processes your data locally, giving you complete control over your privacy. Our advanced algorithms deliver high-quality, coherent outputs that resonate with your personal style. Experience exceptional performance without compromising on security.

Seamless Integration with Cloud Capabilities

For complex tasks, Proxy can securely utilize cloud-based AI services. Our hybrid system ensures your sensitive data remains local while accessing advanced models when needed. Enjoy the best of both worlds: local privacy with the power of the cloud.
