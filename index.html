Proxy

Be There, from Anywhere

What is Proxy?

Imagine stepping into another place, another body, without leaving your home.

Proxy combines advancements in high-speed internet, virtual reality, 3D printing, artificial intelligence, and robotics to create a new way of interacting with the physical world. You will be able to pilot physical robots and vehicles, known as a Proxy, in the real world, from anywhere on the planet, for an affordable price.

Your first Proxy will connect you to the physical world, just as the internet connects you to the digital world. You can inhabit a physical device from anywhere, breaking down barriers and opening up infinite possibilities. By applying technological advancements in several fields, we aim to create an immersive and human experience, both for the person using the Proxy and those interacting with the Proxy device in the physical world.

We are building the future of interaction: Proxy will bridge the gap between the physical and digital world.

How would I use Proxy?

  • Visit Loved Ones: Easily attend birthdays, holidays, and impromptu get-togethers without the hassle and expense of travel.
  • Remote Work: Attend meetings, give presentations, and collaborate with colleagues as if in the same room.
  • Accessible Experiences: Explore museums, attend concerts, or visit new cities from the comfort of your home.
  • Authentic Interactions: Foster genuine connections with people through physical presence, beyond what's possible online.
  • Immersive Learning: Attend classes and workshops with a heightened sense of presence, engaging with instructors and peers.
  • Outsource Errands: Free up time by having someone else remotely handle your errands and chores.
  • Enhance Mobility: Participate in physical events and activities, even with mobility issues.

Proxy will empower you to be anywhere, anytime: you will experience the world like never before.

Is Proxy feasible?

  • The technology is ready: Advancements in VR, robotics, and AI have reached a point where Proxy is now feasible and affordable.
  • The need is real: There is a growing demand for ways to bridge the physical and digital world, especially in post-pandemic society.
  • The team is passionate and determined: We are a diverse group of individuals with expertise in various fields, all driven by the potential of Proxy.
  • We are committed to open source: We believe in collaboration and community involvement, which is why we are building Proxy with the same open-source mindset.

State-of-the-art AI expands what we can do by many magnitudes, allowing us to punch above our weight and deliver on our ambitious goals, while minimizing bureaucracy and complexity.
